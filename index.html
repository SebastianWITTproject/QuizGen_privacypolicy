Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how QuizGen handles your information when you use our app.

Important: QuizGen does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information. All quiz content and user preferences are stored locally on your device.

Data Collection

QuizGen does not collect any personal information from users. We do not gather names, email addresses, device identifiers, location data, or any other personal information.

Local Storage

QuizGen stores quiz content and user preferences locally on your device. This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

AI Processing

QuizGen uses AI services to generate quiz questions from your input text. Your text is processed temporarily and is not stored by the AI service provider.

Children's Privacy

QuizGen is safe for users of all ages as it does not collect any personal information.
